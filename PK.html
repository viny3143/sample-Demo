Konidela Kalyan Babu

BIO

Konidela Pawan Kalyan(born Konidela Kalyan Babu; 2 September 1968[n 1]) is an Indian actor, filmmaker, politician and founder of the Jana Sena Party. He is serving as the 9th Deputy Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh since June 2024[2].
